Multiple and Flexible Pathways - calculating the number of children who need better options

This map shows, for each country, how many spaces would be needed to get all children of upper secondary age on a pathway to complete 12 years of schooling. It uses MICS data for various years. More information can be found here https://data.unicef.org/resources/how-are-children-progressing-through-school/
